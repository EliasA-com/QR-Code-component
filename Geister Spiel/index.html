guess where the Ghost is

Listen carefully, for I have a warning to give you. Behind one of these three doors, a ghost is waiting to claim its next victim. Your task is to guess which door the ghost is hiding behind. You must choose correctly, or else you will fall prey to the ghost's deadly grip. Remember, you only have one chance to make your guess, so choose wisely...
